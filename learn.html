Explore Our Technical Diploma Programs

Computer Information Technology (CIT)

This course focuses on modern computing skills, programming, and networking. Students learn web development, database management, and cybersecurity basics. It prepares students for IT industry roles in both software and hardware. Hands-on labs help in understanding system troubleshooting. Graduates can work as IT technicians or web developers. It’s a perfect diploma for technology enthusiasts.

Electrical Technology

Electrical technology covers power systems, motors, and circuits. Students gain knowledge about residential and industrial wiring. The course includes training in electrical machines and control systems. Safety procedures and practical sessions are a key part. Graduates can work as electricians or maintenance engineers. It builds strong technical and problem-solving skills.

Mechanical Technology

This course focuses on design, manufacturing, and maintenance of machines. Students learn about engines, thermodynamics, and workshop tools. It emphasizes both theory and hands-on practice. Students work with lathes, drilling, and welding machines. Mechanical diploma holders are needed in factories and industries. It’s ideal for those interested in mechanical systems.

Civil Technology

Civil technology deals with design and construction of structures. Students study building materials, surveying, and drawing. Practical training includes site work and project planning. It develops skills in supervision and cost estimation. Graduates can work in public or private construction projects. It’s a great choice for those who love design and field work.

Electronics Technology

This field focuses on circuits, microcontrollers, and electronic devices. Students learn PCB design, digital electronics, and signal systems. It provides knowledge for repairing and testing electronic gadgets. Lab sessions give practical exposure to modern instruments. Graduates can join telecom, automation, or repair industries. It’s perfect for those who love working with gadgets.

Other Technical Courses

GCT also offers short courses and technical diplomas. These include refrigeration, HVAC, auto mechanics, and drafting. Courses are job-oriented and skill-based for quick employment. Students gain both technical and soft skills training. Certification helps in local and overseas job markets. It’s ideal for students seeking practical career paths.
